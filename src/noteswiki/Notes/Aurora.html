#database/analytics - Aurora is a proprietary tech from AWS (not open source) - Postgres and MySQL are both supported as Aurora DB - Aurora is AWS cloud optimized and claims 5x performance improvement over MySQL on RDS and over 3x performance of Postgres on RDS - Aurora Storage automatically grows in increments of 10GB up to 128TB - Aurora Costs around 20% more than RDS but is more efficient - Not in the free tier but RDS is.
