* Infrastructure as a service (IaaS) * Provide building blocks for cloud IT * Provides networking, computers, data storage space * Highest level of flexibility * Easily parallel with traditional on-premises IT * Example: Amazon EC2 * Platform as a service (PaaS) * Removes the need for your organization to manage the underlying infrastructure. * Focus on the deployment and management of your applications. * Example: Elastic beanstalk * Software as a service(SaaS) * Completed produced run and managed by the service provider * Example Rekognition on AWS or Gmail #introduction
